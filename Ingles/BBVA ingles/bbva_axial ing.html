BBVA Axial Tech


I had the opportunity to work on different projects of BBVA Axial Tech, a BBVA company that had a restructuring of its name, brand and vision in 2022.

This company promotes technological transformation through the development of the strategies of the global Engineering area, in which it provides key resources that support the processing, architecture and development of software for BBVA franchises in America and Europe.

DURATION

April 2022 to January 2023 (10 months)

RESPONSIBILITIES

Design intern in the Engineering area:


• Design (UI for social networks and websites)
• Front-end development and content management in WordPress for web pages
• Participation in transversal projects in Latin America and Spain
• Monitoring, execution and development of resources and ideas for projects, internal initiatives and events
• Research with multidisciplinary teams
• Preparation of strategic reports, communications, banners and presentations

TOOLS
WordPress Illustrator Photoshop Premiere Pro After Effects Agile Google Workspace
BRIEF

Because it was a new company in terms of name, brand and objectives, it had certain factors that needed to be innovated:

Lack of up-to-date information and limited visibility

By not having any online communication channel, clients and collaborators lacked relevant and updated information, causing a loss of business opportunities.

Limitations on interaction with customers

The interaction with clients, attraction of talent and with collaborators was limited, and without it, the company lost the connection and commitment with its audience.

STRATEGY

Through several work sessions, the strategy was designed and the KPIs were established to reach a solution based on the problems raised:

Engagement

Achieve an average participation of the staff in events, as well as their interaction through the opening of communication platforms (internal brand portal, social networks and website).

Talent attraction

Brand positioning and improvement of internal and external communication.


RESULTS AND ACHIEVEMENTS

After working closely to bring the company's website and internal portals to life, I received good feedback on my visual designs, which successfully aligned with the technology company's goal and concept. Additionally, internal contributors found the portals experience engaging and convenient.

In the first 5 months from planning, we were able to open the BBVA Axial Tech social network LinkedIn to increase the visibility and importance of the company externally. The company's LinkedIn profile registered followers growth of up to 900% per month organically and more than 260% interactions (reactions + comments + shares) per month.

On the other hand, the official website of BBVA Axial Tech was opened. These victories meant not only an increase in recognition of the importance of the company by internal collaborators and clients, but also a door for our team to improve your work and develop a sense of belonging.


Brand Experience

As part of the objective of improving internal communication, "BBVA Brand Experience" was created. This is a quick-access portal on Google Sites that provides essential brand identity resources to BBVA Axial Tech employees.

The site consists of 8 sections found in the main window, which contain the essential components of the company's identity such as templates for presentations, logos, corporate typography, among others.




Web page

The Engineering area was in charge of creating the initial WordPress template for the BBVA Axial Tech website, which was later delegated to our Brand and Communication team to bring it to life with UI design, copywriting and news updates. .




Social Media Initiatives

I had the opportunity to design, manage and implement different initiatives for social networks with the aim of publicizing the relevant internal achievements of the company. One of the most significant was the #SoyAxtech initiative, which consisted of sharing the meaning of the company through the words of employees to create a feeling of belonging and pride, in addition to encouraging confidence and attracting talent from LinkedIn.



Visual Design

Visual design plays an essential role in a company's social networks and web pages. Not only does it improve aesthetics and brand identity, but it also contributes to effective communication, user experience, and competitive differentiation.

During my internship period, I made different designs for events such as Google Cloud Day in collaboration with BBVA, All Hands (conferences to update relevant information about what happens in a period of time), among others.


CHALLENGES AND LEARNINGS

When working on the design of a company that is well positioned in the market and that already has an established design methodology and system, there are both challenges and opportunities:

Keep consistency

One of the main challenges I faced was maintaining design consistency across all aspects of the business. For this reason, I made sure that all communications and visual designs followed the guidelines established in the BBVA manuals and the design system.

Continuous growth

It was important to keep up with the latest trends and technologies, and constantly look for ways to improve and exceed my work; this involved being flexible and open to feedback from my manager to improve the experience and internal and external communication both on web pages and on social networks and Gmail communications.

Effective communication

Being a well-positioned company, my multidisciplinary team and I had to work on different projects collaborating with people from different geographies around Latin America and Europe. The challenge laid in maintaining effective communication and making sure that everything was aligned with the requirements and objectives of the established tasks.

Optimization and refinement

With a design system already in place, it was easier for me to focus on continually optimizing and refining the design and my soft and hard skills. In this work experience I had the opportunity to learn how to use the basic tools of WordPress and improve my skills in Adobe programs.
